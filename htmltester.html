AT2020PK Streaming & Podcasting Pack

This vocal microphone pack, designed for use by streamers, podcasters and other content creators, includes AT2020 cardioid condenser microphone, ATH-M20x professional monitor headphones and an adjustable desk-mount boom arm threaded with a XLR cable.
